Cooper Siepmann is an accomplished explorer, photographer, and creative director.

    Landscape

Traveling throughout the year to pursue the farthest expanses of Earth, Siepmann works to capture stories that inspire humans to consider their relationship with nature, while promoting the preservation of wild places everywhere.

    Wildlife

Layered by outdoor, travel, adventure, surf, and lifestyle subjects, Siepmann is known for images that are punctuated by untamed, powerful landscapes.

Through social media Cooper strives to share his vision of wild places with millions of people, and to inspire them to explore for themselves
